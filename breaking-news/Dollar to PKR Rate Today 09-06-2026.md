<h2>Dollar to PKR Rate Today Along With Euro, Pound and Riyal Prices in Pakistan</h2>

Foreign currency exchange rates in Pakistan remained mostly stable in the open market on Tuesday, June 9, 2026, with the Pakistani rupee showing slight movement against major international currencies including the US Dollar, Euro, British Pound, UAE Dirham, and Saudi Riyal.

According to the latest market update, the US Dollar was being bought at Rs278.20 and sold at Rs279.45 in the open market. The Euro continued trading above Rs320, while the British Pound remained among the strongest foreign currencies against the Pakistani rupee.

## Key Highlights
- US Dollar traded at Rs278.20 buying
- Dollar selling rate reached Rs279.45
- Euro remained above Rs320
- British Pound stayed strong in open market
- Riyal and Dirham rates remained mostly stable
