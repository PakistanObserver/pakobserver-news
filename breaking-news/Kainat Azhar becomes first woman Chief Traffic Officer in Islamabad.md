# Kainat Azhar Becomes First Woman Chief Traffic Officer in Islamabad

Interior Minister Mohsin Naqvi has approved a major administrative decision in Islamabad Police by appointing the first-ever woman Chief Traffic Officer (CTO) in the federal capital.

Senior Superintendent of Police (SSP) Kainat Azhar has been assigned the responsibilities of CTO Islamabad. She previously served in several key positions, including SSP Operations in Multan, and is known for her extensive field experience and leadership.

## Key Highlights
- Kainat Azhar appointed as Islamabad’s first female CTO
- Approval granted by Interior Minister Mohsin Naqvi
- Previously served as SSP Operations Multan
- Decision seen as an important step for women in policing
