# Polling Underway in Gilgit-Baltistan Amid Tight Security

Polling commenced across Gilgit-Baltistan on Sunday for elections on 24 seats of the regional assembly, with more than 400 candidates contesting under strict security arrangements.

According to the Election Commission, voting started at 8:00 AM and will continue without interruption until 5:00 PM.

## Key Highlights
- Elections on 24 seats
- More than 400 candidates
- Tight security arrangements
- Voting till 5 PM
