# Gilgit-Baltistan GB Election 2026 Results

Results from 17 out of 24 constituencies in the Gilgit-Baltistan Legislative Assembly elections indicate a closely contested political race, with no single party gaining clear dominance so far.

The Pakistan Peoples Party (PPP) has emerged as the leading party with 7 seats, followed by independent candidates securing 5 seats. Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz (PML-N) won 4 seats, while Majlis Wahdat-e-Muslimeen (MWM) secured 1 seat.

## Key Highlights
- PPP leads with 7 seats
- Independent candidates secure 5 seats
- PML-N wins 4 seats
- MWM takes 1 seat
- Tight political competition continues
