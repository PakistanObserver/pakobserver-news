# Fact Check: Punjab Police Female Official’s Alleged Dance Video Goes Viral

A video circulating widely on social media has triggered online debate after a content creator shared footage allegedly showing a female Punjab Police officer dancing.

In the caption accompanying the clip, the creator sarcastically praised Punjab Police and claimed that a “new style of investigation” had been introduced, joking that even hardened criminals would reveal secrets when confronted with such a “special team.”

## Key Highlights
- Viral video sparked debate on social media
- Footage allegedly linked to Punjab Police official
- Users questioned authenticity and context of clip
- Social media reactions continued to grow online
